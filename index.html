Go Above & Beyond Education! Going Above & Beyond Education means providing aggressive supplemental education that is consistent with district practices and structured to meet the needs of students in both reading and mathematics in grades K-12.
SES in Florida In Florida, Above & Beyond Learning provides free individual in home tutoring as well as after school group tutoring at participating school sites. We currently provide services in Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach County.
SES in North Carolina In North Carolina, Above & Beyond Learning provides free individual in home tutoring as well as after school group tutoring at participating school sites. We currently provide services in Gaston County and Robeson County.
Private Tutoring Above & Beyond Learning offers low cost private tutoring conducted in the students home or in a public setting such as libraries and community centers.
